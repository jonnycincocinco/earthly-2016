Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF (VTI)

Expense Ratio: 0.05%

This ETF is designed to provide investors with exposure to the entire U.S. equity market, including small-, mid-, and large-cap growth and value stocks.

Vanguard Total International Stock ETF (VXUS)

Expense Ratio: 0.11%

This ETF provides equity exposure to both developed and emerging international economies. The ETF invests in the stocks of companies domiciled all over the globe, with the exception of the United States.

Vanguard Total Bond Market ETF (BND)

Expense Ratio: 0.06%

This ETF is designed to provide broad exposure to U.S. investment grade bonds. Reflecting this goal, the fund invests about 30% in corporate bonds and 70% in U.S. government bonds of all maturities (short-, intermediate-, and long-term issues).

Vanguard Total International Bond ETF (BNDX)

Expense Ratio: 0.12%

This ETF is designed to provide broad exposure to non-U.S. investment-grade bonds and includes international government, agency, and corporate securities, mostly from developed countries, but also some emerging markets countries.

Vanguard Emerging Markets Government Bond ETF (VWOB)

Expense Ratio: 0.32%

This ETF is designed to provide exposure to government bonds in emerging economies. When combined with BND and BNDX, VWOB helps the client achieve a comprehensive allocation to the world-wide bond market.

Vanguard Short-Term Inflation-Protected Securities ETF (VTIP)

Expense Ratio: 0.07%

This ETF seeks to provide exposure to inflation-protected U.S. Treasury bonds that have a remaining maturity of less than five years and is designed to generate returns more closely correlated with realized inflation over the near term. Earthly recommends shorter duration bonds to help mitigate left-tail risk. Inflation-protected bonds are allocated to conservative portfolios only.

Vanguard Tax-Exempt Bond ETF (VTEB)

Expense Ratio: 0.09%

This ETF is designed to provide broad exposure to U.S. investment-grade municipal bonds. Bonds in this ETF must be a U.S. state, federal district (the District of Columbia) or local government or agency such that interest on the bond is exempt from U.S. federal income taxes. These bonds help U.S. states and municipalities finance infrastructure projects.

Diversification for Maximum Returns and Minimum Risk

We fully diversify your investments across sectors, company sizes and investment styles so that your portfolio earns the best possible returns per level of risk taken.

We examine multiple asset allocation frameworks such as Black-Litterman, Mean-Variance Optimization, Utility Theory and CAPM which help us achieve an optimal portfolio asset allocation.
