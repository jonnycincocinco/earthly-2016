About Earthly

We created Earthly to fill a specific need. The need for a low cost, diversified, sustainable investing solution.

We took the best features from automated and passive investment management and combined them with sustainable investing to create the best way to invest the planet has ever seen.

E6 For A Better Way to Invest

We’ve reimagined the way people invest by creating the E6 framework which serves as the foundation for our platform.

Enable

We enable people of all incomes and walks of life to be able to invest towards causes that make a difference, easily and quickly.

Economize

Investing shouldn’t be expensive. Our investment offerings and services are provided at the lowest possible cost to you.

Elucidate

Our methodology and ratings are fully disclosed. With Earthly(DNA), you know exactly why you own specific companies.

Educate

Knowledge is power, so providing you with the information you need to make informed investment decisions is a top priority.

Embody

Every investor is unique so cookie cutter solutions don’t cut it. Earthly(DNA) tailors its composition to your principles.

Empower

With Earthly(DNA), you hold voting rights to your shares. We vote your shares in accordance with our mission statement.
