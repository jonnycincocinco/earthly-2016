Account Protection

Your account is fully protected by the SIPC up to $500,000 against losses resulting from the failure of Earthly or our custodian Robinhood. In addition, your uninvested cash balance may be swept into one or more participating bank(s) under the FDIC-Insured Sweep Program which allows you to earn interest on uninvested cash.

You should consult the [FDIC website](www.fdic.gov) for further details. SIPC Insurance and the FDIC-Insured Sweep Program does not protect you from the typical ups and downs of the market.
